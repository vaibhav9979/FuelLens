About FuelLens

Smart CNG Compliance & Fuel Station Intelligence Platform

Our Mission

FuelLens is designed to revolutionize the CNG compliance and fuel station management ecosystem by providing a comprehensive platform that ensures safety, compliance, and efficiency across the entire network.

Key Features

  • Real-time Compliance Tracking: Monitor CNG compliance status for all vehicles
  • Automated Reminders: Get timely notifications for upcoming compliance expiries
  • QR Code Verification: Instant compliance verification using QR codes
  • Camera-based Detection: OCR-powered vehicle number plate recognition
  • Station Intelligence: Live load tracking and availability updates
  • Rating System: Community-driven feedback for fuel stations

How It Works

FuelLens connects vehicle owners, fuel station operators, and administrators in a unified platform that streamlines compliance verification, reduces wait times, and ensures safety standards are maintained across all CNG stations.

Security & Privacy

We prioritize the security and privacy of our users' data. All information is encrypted and stored securely with strict access controls to ensure only authorized personnel can access sensitive information.
